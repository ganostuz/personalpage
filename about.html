About

I’m Stanislav, a computer science student interested in making AI systems easier to understand, test, and trust.

I’m in the final year of my BSc at the Faculty of Informatics, Masaryk University. Alongside my studies, I work part-time at Lexical Computing, where I’m building an automated LLM testing suite as my bachelor’s thesis.

Quick history. In my younger years, I was interested in computer graphics and pipelines. I graduated my high school with widowing reference manager created in openGL (more in projects). After that I pivoted into AI sphere.

I’m drawn to the practical side of AI: finding where language-model systems fail, designing evaluations that reveal something useful, and turning the results into better engineering decisions.

I like taking an unclear question, shaping it into a small experiment, and iterating until the result is understandable enough for someone else to use and improve.
